Meet the Founder

The vision behind SMID

👤
✓ Verified Founder

Oluwatosin Abraham Willoughby

Founder & CEO, SMID Platform

"I'm a non-technical founder with a clear vision. I need the right technical partner to bring it to life. SMID isn't just a product — it's a movement to make the internet a place where real people can connect, create, and grow with confidence."

Oluwatosin Abraham Willoughby founded SMID with a singular mission: to eliminate the anonymity that enables online fraud, impersonation, and abuse. Drawing from firsthand experience with the damage caused by fake accounts and unverified identities, he envisioned a universal trust layer that works across every social platform, marketplace, and digital community.

Under his leadership, SMID is building the world's first age-tiered identity verification system — protecting teenagers, empowering young adults, securing professionals, and validating businesses through a single, unified protocol.
